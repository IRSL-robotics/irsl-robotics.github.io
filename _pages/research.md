We are interested in general robotic problems, but particularly in physical interaction problems with robot mobilities (fixed, wheeled, aerial, semi-aerial, etc.). When attempting to combine physical interaction problems with mobility, a number of challenges arise in wide areas of robotics research including design, modeling, control, state estimation, perception, cognition, and planning. We develop intelligent robots with practically appealing scenarios in mind.
